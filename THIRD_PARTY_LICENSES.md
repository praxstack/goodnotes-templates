## Important — LGPL notice for `libvips` (via `sharp`)

`sharp` ships a pre-built `libvips` binary (`@img/sharp-libvips-*`) which is
distributed under **LGPL-3.0-or-later**. `sharp` itself is Apache-2.0.

This project uses `sharp` only at build time (SVG → PNG rasterization). PNGs
produced by libvips are **data** and not derivative works of libvips under the
LGPL-3 dynamic-link exception. Shipping the generated PNGs under MIT / CC BY 4.0
is therefore compatible.

If you ever statically link or vendor the libvips binary, you must comply with
LGPL-3-or-later: either release your linking layer under an LGPL-compatible
license, or provide a mechanism for end users to relink against a modified
libvips. See https://www.gnu.org/licenses/lgpl-3.0.en.html.
