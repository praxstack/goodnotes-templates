The Praxis Ledger is a full-year paginated journal built from a single profile
file. It renders at 300 DPI, splices cleanly, and self-heals across leap years.
Twelve themes ship at zero runtime cost — swap and download.

## Why this pack

Fixed 130-page pagination, eight archetype pages that repeat with intent, and
the only CLI that speaks `profile.json` → PDF in under 90 seconds locally.

## What's inside

- Weekly spreads (Mon-start) with intention + review
- Monthly dashboards with habit grid
- Year-at-a-glance index
- 60 archetype stickers (mood, flip, wins-jar, frog)
- 12 ready-to-use themes
