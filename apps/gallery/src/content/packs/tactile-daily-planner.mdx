Claymorphic A4 daily planner with a 14 mm binder spine and seven skeuomorphic metal rings. Today's Flow is an hourly time-block list; supporting widgets cover mood, hydration, movement, three priorities, a gratitude cloud, and a no-punishment streak counter. Self-contained HTML — no network calls at print time.
