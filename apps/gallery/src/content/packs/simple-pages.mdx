Six utility A4 pages in a single self-contained HTML file and a single 6-page PDF. The kitchen drawer of the library — paper you reach for when you just need to write, sketch, or notate.

## Pages

1. **Lined** — 7 mm ruled, 28 lines, clay margin rule
2. **Grid** — 5 mm Swiss-grid, hairline sage, heavy every 25 mm
3. **Dot grid** — 5 mm dots, muted amber (the bullet-journal standard)
4. **Blank** — cream paper with corner brackets and a hairline border
5. **Isometric** — 30°/60°/90° lattice at 5 mm for technical sketching
6. **Music staff** — eight five-line staves with treble-clef anchor
